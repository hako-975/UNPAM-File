History

PT Hako Hoodie was started on June 28, 2022, which aims to meet the fashion needs of people in Indonesia, especially for young people. at the beginning of its establishment this company was managed by Mr. Andri Firman Saputra.

With ownership number and business license, PT Hako Hoodie has been registered as a company. In 2022. PT Hako Hoodie received support from investors in working capital, technology and human resources, in the following year our company received an award from Jakarta Fashion Week.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Providing the best quality and price by prioritizing consumers to become the best hoodie fashion company at home and abroad.

Mission

  • Recruiting employees who have the ability to compete locally and internationally.
  • Produce with quality basic materials.
  • Providing the best service by prioritizing consumers.
  • Presenting an authentic and unique design.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I exchange my product?

    All products that have been purchased cannot be exchanged.

  • Are all products available?

    Yes, all products are in stock and ready to ship.

  • What if the goods sent are damaged?

    You can request a refund/refund for damaged items. Contact our E-mail or WhatsApp.

  • When will my order be shipped?

    We will ship your order as soon as possible. Orders received until 12:00 WIB will be delivered on the same day, but orders received after that time will be shipped the next day.
